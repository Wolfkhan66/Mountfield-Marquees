Marquees for hire in Sussex and Kent

Based in Robertsbridge, we supply a selection of marquees and tents in various styles and sizes suitable for events ranging from smaller birthdays and parties, to larger occasions such as weddings and festivals.

On request, we also stock benches, tables, flooring, lighting, sound, and electrical equipment for installation.

Enquire about our vintage Rajasthani tents and clear span marquees, with optional panoramic roofs.

Available all year round
